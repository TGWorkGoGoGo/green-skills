# Integration Style

Use these rules when CNKI results are directly integrated into a Deep Research draft.

## Default Organizing Principle

Do not use `国内外研究` as the default index for inserted or rewritten prose.
Instead, organize integrated material around the argument already being made in the paragraph.

Preferred indexes include:

- research viewpoint
- mechanism
- identification strategy
- empirical context
- debate or tension
- boundary condition
- China-specific institutional setting

## What To Avoid

Avoid stock transitions such as:

- `国内外研究表明`
- `国外研究主要关注...，国内研究则...`
- `相较于国外文献，国内文献...`

Use those only when the user explicitly asks for a comparative domestic-foreign review structure.

## Recommended Stitching Moves

- extend an existing claim with a China-specific mechanism
- contrast a broad English claim with a Chinese institutional qualification
- insert a Chinese empirical angle that sharpens the paragraph's existing cut
- surface a Chinese-side debate rather than merely noting that domestic studies also exist

## Example Indexing Language

Prefer expressions such as:

- `从流动性约束的视角看`
- `围绕技术创新这一机制`
- `在县域微观证据层面`
- `就城乡收入差距这一结果变量而言`
- `中国语境下的一个关键争议是`
- `既有研究的分歧主要在于`

## Patch-Mode Goal

After integration, the paragraph should read as a tighter argument with stronger China-facing support, not as an added survey note announcing that domestic research also exists.
